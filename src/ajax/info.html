This is Stargazer

This system watches over all travelling and static space objects in our Solar system. Everything comes here from the asteroid fields to simple shuttles and satellites. With this system you can receive superior intel on any object, including it's ownership rights, state, contents and carried cargo, superficial position and missions, every, from planned ones to those completed.
    To use it everything you need to do is:
  • Select the tab "Gaze'Em"
  • Select the information you require and recieve it.
  • You may choose additional options for it's view solution.
